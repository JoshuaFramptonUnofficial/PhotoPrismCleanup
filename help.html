PhotoPrismCleanup Help & Troubleshooting

Welcome! Here’s how to use PhotoPrismCleanup:

  • On the login screen, enter your SSH details and hit Connect (or press Enter).
  • Use the to Delete, to Undo (Z or ↓), or to Keep each photo/video.
  • Toggle between Light 🌞 and Dark 🌜 mode using the switch in the top toolbar.
  • Click the Help button here at any time to reopen this page.
  • In the Settings tab you can adjust:
    • Originals folder
    • Thumbnail-cache folder
    • Which media types to show (Photos/Videos)
  • Use “Save Progress” or simply Exit/Logout to persist your last index and settings.

Troubleshooting

Connection Errors: If you see Cannot reach host or Remote folder not found, verify your IP, port, and folder path.

White Screen: A loading overlay appears when files load. If it hangs, check your network or restart the app.
